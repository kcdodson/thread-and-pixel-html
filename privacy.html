Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

Thread & Pixel ("we," "us," or "our") is a web design and development company based in Chattanooga, Tennessee. Our website is threadandpixel.com. Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@threadandpixel.com.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business name
  • The service you're interested in
  • Any message you choose to include

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or fingerprinting scripts. We do not collect data passively as you browse this site.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We will:

  • Reply to your message and answer your questions
  • Follow up about a potential project if you've expressed interest

We will not add you to a mailing list, sell your information to third parties, or use it for any purpose unrelated to your inquiry.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form uses Web3Forms to process and deliver form submissions. When you submit the contact form, your data is transmitted to Web3Forms' servers and then forwarded to our email. Please review Web3Forms' privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of serving those font files. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and manage a potential business relationship. If you would like your information removed, contact us at hello@threadandpixel.com and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided to us. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@threadandpixel.com.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
